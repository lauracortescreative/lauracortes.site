Personal Project: Wild Cities AR

üì± AR Story App iOS + Android

üí° Wild Cities AR is an immersive, voice-activated storytelling app for iOS and Android. It boosts kids' eco-empathy by empowering them to develop their virtual forest right in their backyards - or living rooms. Climate change, deforestation, and urbanization affect the world in ways that impact us all. Still, the loss of natural habitats and wildlife is invisible to many urban kids.


Wild Cities AR allows kids to experience nature anywhere, teaching them about urban ecology, inspiring them to take responsibility for local wildlife, and empowering them to help fight climate change. By helping restore The Magic Tree, kids will discover their forests' hidden secrets and learn how to work together to care for our planet.
