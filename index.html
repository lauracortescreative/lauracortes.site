SIREN

3D Visuals & Motion Design

SIREN — 3D Motion Visuals

3D Visuals & Motion Design

Siren is a personal project exploring 3D motion design and visual storytelling. The series investigates themes of transformation and digital identity through abstract visual language.


Using Cinema 4D and Octane Renderer, I created a series of animated sequences that blend organic and digital elements. The project was featured in Adobe's Behance Graphic Design gallery and received recognition at the Barcelona Design Week.

TERRA MATER

Exhibition Design

TERRA MATER — Exhibition

Exhibition Design

Terra Mater was an exhibition design project for the Barcelona Museum of Contemporary Art, focusing on environmental awareness and our relationship with nature.


The challenge was to create an immersive experience that educated visitors on environmental issues while inspiring positive action. Working with a multidisciplinary team, I developed the visual identity, environmental graphics, and interactive installations that guided visitors through the narrative journey.

GRADIENT EXPLORATIONS

Visual Design

GRADIENT EXPLORATIONS

Visual Design

A series of experimental visual designs exploring the creative potential of color gradients. This ongoing project investigates how gradients can evoke emotions, create depth, and communicate complex ideas through simple visual means.


The project includes digital artwork, print applications, and motion studies that showcase different approaches to gradient design. Select pieces from this series have been used in commercial projects for clients in the fashion and tech industries.

TYPE SPECIMENS

Typography

TYPE SPECIMENS

Typography

Type Specimens is a personal exploration of typography and editorial design. The project examines how different typefaces can establish mood, impact readability, and define the character of design work.


The collection includes a series of type specimens, editorial layouts, and typographic posters that showcase both classic and contemporary typefaces. Each design highlights the unique characteristics and potential applications of the featured typeface.

MOTION GRAPHICS REEL

MOTION GRAPHICS REEL

Motion Design

A compilation of motion design work created for various commercial and personal projects. The reel showcases my approach to animation, timing, and visual storytelling through motion.


Projects featured in the reel include broadcast graphics, title sequences, brand animations, and experimental motion studies. Each project demonstrates different techniques and styles, from minimalist 2D animation to complex 3D work.

BRAND IDENTITY PROJECTS

BRAND IDENTITY PROJECTS

Branding

A selection of brand identity projects for clients across various industries. Each project involved developing comprehensive visual systems that communicate the client's values and connect with their target audience.


My branding process focuses on understanding the client's business objectives, market position, and audience needs before developing visual concepts. The final deliverables typically include logo design, typography, color palettes, and brand applications across print and digital media.

ABOUT

I'm Laura Cortes, a Visual Designer with a passion for creating meaningful visual experiences. My work spans multiple disciplines including digital design, motion graphics, and exhibition design.


With a background in Fine Arts and a Master's in Design from ELISAVA School of Design in Barcelona, I approach each project with a blend of artistic sensitivity and strategic thinking. My design process is centered on research, experimentation, and attention to detail.


I've collaborated with clients and studios across Europe, including Estudio Mariscal, Barcelona Design Museum, and various technology startups. My work has been recognized in publications like IdN Magazine and Communication Arts.


When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring photography, attending design exhibitions, and seeking inspiration in Barcelona's vibrant cultural scene.


Based in Barcelona, Spain.
