Laura Cortes [she/her] [pronounced "Lah-ooh-ra Cour-tsh"] is an independent creative director whose career has followed the evolution of interactive storytelling across continents and platforms. Wild Cities AR

AR STORY APP

Personal Project: Wild Cities AR

üì± AR Story App iOS + Android

üí° Wild Cities AR is an immersive, voice-activated storytelling app for iOS and Android. It boosts kids' eco-empathy by empowering them to develop their virtual forest right in their backyards - or living rooms. Climate change, deforestation, and urbanization affect the world in ways that impact us all. Still, the loss of natural habitats and wildlife is invisible to many urban kids.


Wild Cities AR allows kids to experience nature anywhere, teaching them about urban ecology, inspiring them to take responsibility for local wildlife, and empowering them to help fight climate change. By helping restore The Magic Tree, kids will discover their forests' hidden secrets and learn how to work together to care for our planet.


Wild Cities AR was created by Toronto's design and experience team at Jam3 in collaboration with a-Bahn, the Canadian Media Fund, the country of Luxembourg, and the City of Toronto.

ABOUT

Laura's journey began in Lisbon, Portugal, where she earned her BA in Communication Design before expanding her expertise with an MA in New Media and a Diploma in Digital Design from VFS. Her professional path has taken her through Vancouver, London, and Toronto, starting with educational flash games for textbook CDROMs in 2011. Her career trajectory includes leadership roles at Unit9, Nexus, and Jam3, where she developed interactive storytelling projects using AR and web technologies for TikTok, Samsung, eBay, Amazon, Est√©e Lauder, Pepsi, and The NFB. She also headed the digital department at Warner Bros. Records (UK), driving digital storytelling and innovation for artist campaigns including Muse, Liam Gallagher, and Dua Lipa. Beyond her commercial work, Laura remains committed to community service through multiple mentorship programs across Portugal, UK, and Canada, while providing pro-bono services to nonprofits such as A Woman's Worth and One Cause a Day. Fluent in Portuguese, Spanish, and English, Laura brings a truly global perspective to every project she undertakes.
